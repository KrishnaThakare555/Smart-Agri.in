HOW TO FIND A PH LEVEL OF YOUR SOIL
Step 1 : Take a tspoon of your soil
Step 2 : Take 2 tspoon of distilled water
Step 3 : Mix it well for 10 sec
Step 4 : Buy a litmus paper { Ph test indecator }
Step 5 : Keep Ph paper / Litmus paper in the bowl for 5-10 sec { Where you mixed distilled water and soil } " do not put litmus paper inside the soil put in over the water "
Step 6 : Match the colour accourding to the guide given with the litmus paper and find the Ph number and find which plant is sutabke for your Home garden
